About Me

Who I Am

I'm Galana Garoma, a dedicated Odoo developer and functional consultant based in Ethiopia. My journey in technology started with a BSc in Computer Engineering, which gave me strong fundamentals in software architecture, algorithms, and system design.

I deepened my Odoo expertise through Odoo 19 Technical Development training at Skill Bridge Institute of Technology, mastering modern Odoo patterns including the OWL (Odoo Web Library) JavaScript framework for building reactive frontend components.

My approach combines technical depth with functional understanding. Whether it's a complex multi-company payroll customization, a custom OWL dashboard, or a full Odoo implementation — I bring precision and passion to every project.

I'm comfortable across the full Odoo stack: Python ORM models, PostgreSQL optimization, QWeb templates, OWL components, and Docker deployment on Ubuntu servers.
