About Me

Who I Am

I'm Galana Garoma, a dedicated Odoo developer and functional consultant based in Ethiopia. My journey in technology started with a BSc in Computer Engineering, which gave me strong fundamentals in software architecture, algorithms, and system design.

I deepened my Odoo expertise through Odoo 19 Technical Development training at Skill Bridge Institute of Technology, where I mastered modern Odoo patterns including the OWL (Odoo Web Library) JavaScript framework for building reactive frontend components.

My approach combines technical depth with functional understanding — I don't just write code, I understand business processes and craft ERP solutions that genuinely solve problems. Whether it's a complex multi-company payroll customization, a custom reporting module, or a full Odoo implementation, I bring precision and passion to every project.

I'm comfortable across the full stack: from Python ORM models and PostgreSQL query optimization to QWeb templates, OWL components, and Docker deployment on Ubuntu servers.
